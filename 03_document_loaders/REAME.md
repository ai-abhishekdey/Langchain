## LangChain Document Loaders

**Document loaders** provide a standard interface for reading data from different sources. Refer to [LangChain Document Loaders](https://docs.langchain.com/oss/python/integrations/document_loaders) for different types of document loaders supported by LangChain

## Some of the most frequently used document loaders

1. [CSV Loader](https://docs.langchain.com/oss/python/integrations/document_loaders/csv)

2. [Text Loader](https://docs.langchain.com/oss/javascript/integrations/document_loaders/file_loaders/text#integration-details)

3. [PDF Loader](https://docs.langchain.com/oss/python/integrations/document_loaders/pypdfloader)

4. [JSON Loader](https://docs.langchain.com/oss/python/integrations/document_loaders/json)
